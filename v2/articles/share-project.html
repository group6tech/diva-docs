Before you begin

Before sharing a new project, ensure:

  • a user has created a project in Avid Media Composer on the correct drive, and
  • the user has closed the project in Avid Media Composer.

Share a project

  1. On the DIVA Fusion menu, click Projects.
  2. Click the relevant project's blue button.
  3. You will be asked to confirm that you want to share the project. Click Share.
  4. After a few moments the blue button should disappear next the project you just shared, and you should see a notification message such as:

    Share multiple projects

    You can also share more than one project at a time.
    1. On the DIVA Fusion menu, click Projects.
    2. Use the checkboxes to select the projects to share. You can use the filter and search tools on the left to help find the projects.
    3. Click the Share button below the project list.
    4. A dialog asks you to confirm that you want to share the selected projects. If any of the selected projects are already shared, they exluded from this list.
    5. Click the Share button in to share listed the projects.
