Health check a project

If users are having problems opening other users’ bins, you can repair a project to fix the problems. Repairing a project will restore links and permissions.

Repair a project

  1. On the DIVA Fusion menu, click Projects.

  2. Click the relevant shared project’s icon.

  3. You will be asked if you want to repair the project. Click Repair.

Result: A job that repairs the project is created, queued and run in the background. Depending on the size of the project, this may take anything from a few seconds to 1 minute.

Tip: You can health check multiple projects at once by using the checkboxes to select the projects, then click the Repair button below the project list.
