Updating the Email Details that are used to Send Notifications

Notifications are automatically emailed to administrators' email addresses from an email address specified in DIVA Fusion. Examples of notifications that are emailed include:

  • Notification of a job that has failed
  • Notification of a drive that has reached 50 % full
  • [?Anything Else? ?], and
  • [?Anything Else? ?]

You can easily update the following two details:

  • The sender's email address.
  • Note: This is the email address that is used when emailed notifications are automatically sent from DIVA Fusion.
  • The sender's name that is displayed when the recipient receives your emails. [?Is this correct? ?]

Before You Begin

Before updating the the email details that are used to send notifications, ensure DIVA Fusion is running and you are signed in.

To update the email details that are used to send notifications:

  1. From DIVA Fusion's System menu, click Email.
    • Result: The default sender address and default sender name are displayed.
  2. Where relevant, overtype the corresponding fields with an up-to-date email address and/or name.
  3. Click 'Save Email Configuration'.
    • Result: Your changes are saved.
