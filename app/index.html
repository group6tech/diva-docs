Welcome to DIVA Help

With DIVA:

  • multiple users can work simultaneously across multiple servers and drives
  • workflow is improved thanks to an environment optimised for editors
  • scalability is limited only by network speed and connectivity
  • favourites can be setup to access projects with a click so you can get cutting quickly
  • the configuration of storage drives is customisable
  • comprehensive backups to protect your work are affordable, and
  • you receive 24/7 international remote support.

This online help has been designed to make finding the information that you need as easy as possible. All major topic categories are accessible from every page by using the tabs (above). The information that you seek should be little more than a few of clicks away.

Comprehensive instructions on how to perform DIVA tasks have been provided for you to get up to speed quickly and efficiently, and to minimise any potential downtime.
