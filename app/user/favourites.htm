Favourites

Lorem & Ipsum

Favourites provide you with easy access to the drives you use most often. A favourite is a customised collection of DIVA drives that you can mount all at once. This saves you time because by using your favourites, you don't have to locate and mount the drives you use most often one by one. Is this correct?

Favourites are available to all users. That is, any favourites you create are available to other users, and any favourites set up by someone else will be available to you. Of course, your ability to access and work with particular drives is dependent on whether your user account has been granted access.
