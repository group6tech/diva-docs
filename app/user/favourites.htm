Favourites

Favourites provide you with easy access to the drives you use most often. A favourite is a customised grouping of drives. With a favourite, you can mount a set of drives in one go; you don't have to locate and mount drives one at a time.

Favourites can be made available to all DIVA Client users or a particular group of users. To set up a favourite, see your administrator.

The following picture shows the detail that is displayed to the right of the favourites list when a favourite is selected. That is, if you click a favourite, the list of drives that have been assigned to the favourite are listed to the right.

Using a Favourite to Mount a Set of Drives

Before You Begin

Before mounting a set of drives with a favourite, ensure you are signed into DIVA Client:

To use a favourite to mount a set of drives:

  1. On the DIVA Client menu, click Favourites
    • Result: The Favourites page is displayed.
  2. Click the relevant favourite's green + button.

    • Result: The drives that have been assigned to the favourite, and that you have permission to see or use, are mounted.
    • Note: Alternatively, you can click the favourite, then click the Load Favourite button.

What's Next?

If you want to see which drives are mounted, on the DIVA Client menu, click Drives.
