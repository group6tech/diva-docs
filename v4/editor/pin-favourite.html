Pin a favourite

If you find that you are using one or more favourites frequently, you can pin these favourites so that they appear at the top of your favourites list for faster access.

Note: You can pin multiple favourites. They will be listed in alphabetical order above all other favourites.

Pin a favourite

  1. On the DIVA Client menu, click Favourites.

  2. Find the favourite you want to pin in the favourites list.

  3. As you hover your cursor over the favourite you want to pin, the button will appear.

  4. Click the relevant favourite’s button. Once pinned the icon will become a solid , and move to the top of the list.

Result: The selected favourite has been pinned and will appear at the top of your favourites list. Pinned favourites have a icon next to them.

What’s Next?

If you want to unpin a favourite, click on the button on the pinned favourite to unpin it.
